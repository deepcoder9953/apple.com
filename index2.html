    PORTABLE DESIGN

    Designed to go places.

    Super light and just over 1 cm thin MacBook Air fits easily into your life and is built with the planet in mind. MacBook Air with M3 is made with 50% recycled materials — an Apple first. And all MacBook Air laptops have a durable recycled aluminium enclosure.

    Two perfectly portable sizes. The 34.46 cm (13.6″) model is the ultimate on‑the‑go laptop, and the 38.91 cm (15.3″) model gives you more space onscreen for multitasking.2

    Four stellar colours. Your options are out of this world — and each one comes with a matching MagSafe charging cable.
